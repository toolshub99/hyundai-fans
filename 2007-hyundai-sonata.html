2007 Hyundai Sonata: Complete Review, Fuel Economy, and Value Analysis

Discover the refined 2007 Hyundai Sonata with its updated styling, improved features, and exceptional value that continues to compete strongly in the midsize sedan segment.

2007 Hyundai Sonata: Refined and Improved

The 2007 Hyundai Sonata builds upon the successful redesign of the 2006 model with subtle refinements and additional features. This model year continues to offer Hyundai's commitment to quality and value, maintaining its competitive position in the midsize sedan market.

Continuing on Hyundai's NF platform, the 2007 Sonata offers refined driving dynamics, enhanced safety, and a spacious interior. The cabin features quality materials and updated technology that delivers impressive value for the price.

Key Features of the 2007 Model

  • Refined exterior styling with new grille design
  • Updated interior with improved materials
  • Available V6 engine with 235 horsepower
  • Standard electronic stability control
  • Six standard airbags
  • Enhanced audio and infotainment options

Hyundai Sonata SE: Sporty Features and Great Value

The Hyundai Sonata SE trim offers a sportier take on the Sonata with additional performance and styling features. Positioned between the base GLS and premium Limited trims, the SE adds sport-tuned suspension and unique styling elements.

SE Exclusive Features:

  • 17-inch alloy wheels with sport design
  • Sport-tuned suspension
  • Leather-wrapped steering wheel and shift knob
  • Unique front and rear fascias
  • Fog lights
  • Steering wheel audio controls

Respectable Fuel Economy: Hyundai Sonata MPG

The 2007 Sonata delivers competitive fuel efficiency for a midsize sedan, with the base four-cylinder engine providing a great balance of power and economy. The available V6 model offers strong performance while maintaining reasonable fuel consumption.

Engine/Trim City MPG Highway MPG Combined MPG
2.4L 4-cylinder (GLS, SE) 24 34 28
3.3L V6 (Limited) 20 30 24

The Sonata's fuel economy numbers remained competitive for its class in 2007, with the four-cylinder model offering particularly good efficiency for daily commuting and highway driving.

Advanced Technology & Safety Features

The 2007 Sonata continued to offer well-equipped standard safety features that were often optional on competitors. Electronic Stability Control and six airbags remained standard across all trims - a significant advantage in its class.

Standard Safety Features:

  • Electronic Stability Control (ESC)
  • Traction Control System (TCS)
  • Anti-lock Braking System (ABS)
  • Six airbags (front, side, curtain)
  • Active front head restraints

Available premium features included leather upholstery, heated seats, a sunroof, and an upgraded audio system with CD changer. The 2007 model also introduced XM Satellite Radio as an available option.

Why Choose the 2007 Hyundai Sonata?

The 2007 Hyundai Sonata stands out in the midsize sedan segment with its refined styling, comprehensive standard safety features, and exceptional value. Its combination of attractive design, comfortable interior, and competitive pricing made it a compelling choice for sedan shoppers in 2007.

Whether you're considering the well-equipped base GLS model, the sporty SE, or the premium Limited with V6 power, the Sonata delivers a quality experience that's difficult to match in its price range.
