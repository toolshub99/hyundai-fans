2022 Hyundai Sonata: Stylish Design, Advanced Tech & Impressive Efficiency

Explore the 2022 Hyundai Sonata's sophisticated design, cutting-edge technology, and impressive fuel economy that make it a standout in the midsize sedan segment.

2022 Hyundai Sonata: A Design Masterpiece

The 2022 Hyundai Sonata continues to impress with its sophisticated "Sensuous Sportiness" design language that turns heads on the road. With its sleek profile, dramatic lighting signatures, and premium interior, the Sonata elevates the midsize sedan experience.

This model year brought subtle refinements to an already impressive package, including enhanced standard safety features and technology upgrades across the lineup. The Sonata's striking appearance is matched by its comfortable, tech-forward cabin and impressive driving dynamics.

Key Features for 2022

  • Sensuous Sportiness design with parametric jewel pattern grille
  • Available 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster
  • Hyundai Digital Key smartphone integration
  • Enhanced Hyundai SmartSense safety suite
  • Available 1.6L Turbo and 2.5L engine options
  • Impressive hybrid efficiency with up to 52 MPG combined

Hyundai Sonata Hybrid: Efficiency Meets Style

The 2022 Sonata Hybrid models deliver exceptional fuel economy without compromising on style or performance. Featuring Hyundai's innovative Solar Roof System on higher trims, the Hybrid can actually recharge its battery using solar energy, extending its electric-only range.

Hybrid Exclusive Features:

  • Solar roof system (Limited trim)
  • Up to 52 MPG combined
  • Active Shift Control technology
  • Hybrid-specific 16-inch or 17-inch alloy wheels
  • Unique hybrid badging
  • Drive Mode Select with EV mode

Impressive Fuel Economy: 2022 Sonata MPG

The 2022 Sonata offers a range of powertrains to suit different driving preferences, from efficient daily commuting to more spirited performance. The hybrid models stand out with exceptional fuel economy numbers that rival dedicated hybrid vehicles.

Powertrain/Trim City MPG Highway MPG Combined MPG
2.5L 4-cylinder (SE, SEL) 28 38 32
1.6L Turbo (SEL Plus, N Line) 27 37 31
2.5L Hybrid Blue 50 54 52
2.5L Hybrid (SEL, Limited) 45 51 47

The Sonata Hybrid Blue stands out with its impressive 52 MPG combined rating, making it one of the most fuel-efficient non-plug-in vehicles in its class while maintaining the Sonata's signature style and comfort.

N Line: Sporty Performance

For driving enthusiasts, the Sonata N Line adds a sporty edge to the sophisticated sedan. With its more powerful turbocharged engine, sport-tuned suspension, and aggressive styling cues, the N Line delivers enhanced performance without sacrificing daily comfort.

N Line Exclusive Features

  • 2.5L Turbo GDI engine with 290 hp
  • Sport-tuned suspension
  • N Line exterior styling with dark chrome accents
  • Dual exhaust with chrome tips
  • Sport seats with N Line logo
  • Paddle shifters
  • 19-inch alloy wheels

Current Market Value & Buying Considerations

As a used vehicle in 2025, the 2022 Hyundai Sonata represents excellent value with modern features, strong reliability, and attractive pricing compared to newer models.

Current Used Market Pricing (2025)

  • SE/SEL (Low mileage): $19,000 - $22,000
  • SEL Plus (Low mileage): $22,000 - $25,000
  • Limited (Low mileage): $26,000 - $30,000
  • Hybrid models: Add $1,500 - $3,000
  • N Line: $24,000 - $28,000

Note: Prices vary based on location, condition, mileage, and maintenance history.

What to Look For When Buying:

  • Check for complete service records and maintenance history
  • Verify all electronic features work properly, especially the infotainment system
  • Inspect for any signs of previous accident damage
  • Test drive to ensure smooth transmission operation
  • Check for any recalls and verify they've been addressed
  • For hybrid models, verify battery health and charging system

Technology & Safety Features

The 2022 Sonata comes packed with advanced technology and safety features that were cutting-edge for its time and remain impressive today:

Notable Technology & Safety

  • Hyundai SmartSense: Comprehensive suite of safety features including Forward Collision-Avoidance Assist, Lane Keeping Assist, and Blind-Spot Collision-Avoidance Assist
  • Digital Key: Use your smartphone as a vehicle key (Limited trim)
  • Remote Smart Parking Assist: Can move the vehicle forward or backward remotely using the key fob
  • 10.25-inch Touchscreen: Responsive display with navigation and smartphone integration
  • Bose Premium Audio: 12-speaker system for exceptional sound quality
  • Bluelink Connected Services: Remote start, vehicle status, and safety services via smartphone app

Why Choose the 2022 Hyundai Sonata?

The 2022 Hyundai Sonata stands out in the competitive midsize sedan segment with its head-turning design, premium interior, advanced technology, and impressive fuel efficiency. Whether you're considering the standard gasoline models, the ultra-efficient hybrid variants, or the sporty N Line, the Sonata delivers a compelling package.

As a used vehicle, the 2022 Sonata offers exceptional value with modern features, strong reliability ratings, and lower depreciation compared to some competitors. Its comprehensive warranty coverage (5-year/60,000-mile new vehicle, 10-year/100,000-mile powertrain) provides additional peace of mind for used car buyers.
