2015 Hyundai Sonata Sport: Complete Guide & Specifications

The 2015 Hyundai Sonata Sport represents the perfect balance between everyday comfort and sporty character, featuring distinctive styling cues, enhanced driving dynamics, and premium features that set it apart from standard Sonata models.
