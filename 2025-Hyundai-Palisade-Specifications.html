2025 Hyundai Palisade Specifications: Complete Technical Details

Explore comprehensive technical specifications for the 2025 Hyundai Palisade including detailed dimensions, powertrain specifications, towing capacity, and complete feature breakdown across all trim levels.

Overview & Key Specifications

The 2025 Hyundai Palisade continues as Hyundai's flagship three-row SUV, offering premium features, advanced technology, and robust capability. This comprehensive specifications guide covers every technical detail you need to know about this sophisticated family SUV.

Quick Specifications

  • Seating Capacity: 7 or 8 passengers
  • Engine: 3.8L V6 GDI
  • Horsepower: 291 hp @ 6,000 rpm
  • Torque: 262 lb-ft @ 5,200 rpm
  • Towing Capacity: 5,000 lbs (with tow package)
  • Cargo Volume: 18.0 - 86.4 cu ft
