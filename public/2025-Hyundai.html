2025 Hyundai Elantra: Review, MPG, Lease Deals & SEL Sport Features

Discover the refined 2025 Hyundai Elantra featuring impressive fuel economy, attractive lease offers, and the sporty SEL Sport trim that delivers exceptional value in the compact sedan segment.

Sophisticated Redesign: Modern Compact Excellence

The 2025 Hyundai Elantra continues to set the standard for compact sedans with its sophisticated design, advanced technology, and exceptional value proposition. Building on the successful current generation, the 2025 model introduces subtle refinements and enhanced features that maintain its competitive edge.

With its parametric dynamics design language, the Elantra offers one of the most distinctive and modern appearances in its class, combining aerodynamic efficiency with striking visual appeal that stands out in a sea of conservative competitors.

2025 Model Year Updates

  • Refreshed front fascia with new grille design
  • Enhanced standard safety features across all trims
  • Updated infotainment system with faster processor
  • New available exterior color options
  • Improved sound insulation for quieter cabin
  • Expanded standard driver assistance features

Exterior Design: Parametric Dynamics

The 2025 Elantra features Hyundai's signature parametric dynamics design with sharp character lines, a fastback-like profile, and sophisticated details that give it a premium presence uncommon in the compact segment.

The SEL Sport trim adds distinctive styling elements including unique front and rear fascias, sportier wheels, and additional black accents that enhance its athletic appearance.

Exterior Design Features:

  • Parametric jewel pattern grille
  • LED headlights and daytime running lights
  • Fastback-inspired silhouette
  • Chrome window accents
  • Integrated rear spoiler
  • Dual exhaust tips (SEL Sport)

Interior: Tech-Focused Cabin

Inside, the 2025 Elantra offers a modern, driver-focused cabin with premium materials and advanced technology. The interior design emphasizes both style and functionality with thoughtful storage solutions and comfortable seating.

The SEL Sport trim features sport-inspired interior elements including unique upholstery, contrast stitching, and aluminum sport pedals that enhance the driving experience.

Interior Highlights:

  • Dual 10.25-inch digital displays (SEL Sport and above)
  • Leatherette seating surfaces with contrast stitching
  • Automatic climate control
  • Wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
  • Wireless phone charging
  • Heated front seats
