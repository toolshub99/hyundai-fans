2025 Hyundai Santa Fe: Bold Redesign & Premium Features

Explore the completely redesigned 2025 Hyundai Santa Fe with its bold boxy styling, premium interior, advanced technology features, and comprehensive technical specifications.

Overview & Key Specifications

The 2025 Hyundai Santa Fe represents a radical departure from its predecessor with a bold, boxy design that maximizes interior space while delivering premium features and advanced technology. This comprehensive specifications guide covers every technical detail of this sophisticated midsize SUV.

Quick Specifications

  • Seating Capacity: 5 or 7 passengers
  • Engine: 2.5L Turbo GDI or 1.6L Turbo Hybrid
  • Horsepower: 277 hp (Turbo) / 232 hp (Hybrid)
  • Torque: 311 lb-ft (Turbo) / 271 lb-ft (Hybrid)
  • Towing Capacity: 3,500 lbs (with tow package)
  • Cargo Volume: 22.0 - 72.1 cu ft
