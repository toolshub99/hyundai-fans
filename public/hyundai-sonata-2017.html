2017 Hyundai Sonata: Complete Guide & Specifications

Discover the 2017 Hyundai Sonata - a refined midsize sedan offering sophisticated styling, advanced safety technology, and exceptional value in the competitive family sedan segment.

Overview: Refined Midsize Sedan

The 2017 Hyundai Sonata represents the culmination of Hyundai's commitment to delivering a premium midsize sedan experience at an accessible price point, featuring sophisticated styling, advanced technology, and comprehensive safety features.

Key Features & Highlights

  • Sophisticated Design: Fluidic Sculpture 2.0 design language
  • Advanced Safety: Available Hyundai SmartSense safety suite
  • Spacious Interior: Roomy cabin with premium materials
  • Efficient Powertrains: Multiple engine options with excellent fuel economy
  • Technology: Modern infotainment with Apple CarPlay/Android Auto
  • Value: Premium features at competitive pricing

Safety & Driver Assistance

The 2017 Sonata offers comprehensive safety features with available advanced driver assistance systems.

Safety Features

  • Standard Safety: 6 airbags, electronic stability control, anti-lock brakes
  • Available SmartSense: Forward collision warning with automatic emergency braking
  • Lane Departure Warning: Alerts driver if unintentionally leaving lane
  • Blind Spot Detection: With rear cross-traffic alert
  • Smart Cruise Control: Maintains set distance from vehicle ahead
  • Rearview Camera: Standard on all trims
