Hyundai Kona Comparison: 2023 vs 2020 - Complete Analysis

Discover the significant upgrades and changes between the redesigned 2023 Hyundai Kona and the previous 2020 model - from exterior styling to advanced technology and performance improvements.

Overview: Major Redesign for 2023

The 2023 Hyundai Kona represents a complete redesign with significant improvements over the 2020 model, featuring bolder styling, enhanced technology, and improved performance across all trim levels.

Key Differences at a Glance

  • Exterior: Completely redesigned with larger dimensions and bolder styling
  • Interior: More spacious cabin with premium materials and dual 10.25" screens
  • Technology: Advanced Hyundai SmartSense and wireless connectivity
  • Performance: New 2.0L engine option and improved N Line variant
  • Electric Version: Significantly upgraded range and charging capabilities
  • Value: More standard features at similar price points
