2022-2023 Hyundai Palisade Calligraphy: Ultimate Luxury SUV Review

Discover the 2022-2023 Hyundai Palisade Calligraphy - Hyundai's flagship luxury SUV that combines premium amenities, advanced technology, and exceptional three-row versatility to compete with luxury brands.

Premium Presence: Sophisticated SUV Design

The 2022-2023 Hyundai Palisade Calligraphy represents the pinnacle of Hyundai's SUV lineup, offering a perfect blend of sophisticated styling, premium craftsmanship, and family-friendly functionality. As the top-tier trim, the Calligraphy elevates the Palisade to compete directly with luxury competitors from Acura, Lexus, and Infiniti.

Both the 2022 and 2023 models feature the same premium design and features, with the Palisade receiving a significant refresh for the 2023 model year that enhances its already impressive presence with updated styling and additional standard features.

Calligraphy Exclusive Features

  • Unique 20-inch dark-finish alloy wheels
  • Premium Nappa leather seating surfaces
  • Quilted leather door panel inserts
  • Extended leather-wrapped dashboard
  • Premium suede-like headliner
  • Chrome exterior accents and badging

Exterior Design: Commanding Presence

The Palisade Calligraphy features a bold and sophisticated exterior design that commands attention on the road. The signature cascading grille, full LED lighting, and elegant proportions create a premium appearance that belies its accessible price point.

For 2023, the Palisade received updated front and rear styling with new LED lighting elements, revised bumper designs, and fresh wheel options that enhance its already impressive road presence.

Exterior Design Features:

  • Signature cascading chrome grille
  • Full LED headlights and DRLs
  • LED taillights with new signature lighting (2023)
  • 20-inch dark machine-finished alloy wheels
  • Chrome window surrounds and roof rails
  • Power-folding side mirrors with approach lights

Interior: Luxury Lounge Experience

Inside, the Palisade Calligraphy offers a truly premium environment that rivals luxury competitors. The cabin features exceptional materials, thoughtful details, and advanced technology throughout all three rows.

The 2023 model introduces enhanced interior options including new color schemes and additional standard features, maintaining the Palisade's position as one of the most luxurious vehicles in its class.

Interior Highlights:

  • Premium Nappa leather upholstery
  • Quilted leather door panel inserts
  • 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster
  • 12.3-inch touchscreen infotainment system
  • Heated and ventilated first and second-row seats
  • Premium 12-speaker Harman Kardon audio system

Powertrain and Performance

The Palisade Calligraphy features a refined powertrain that delivers smooth, confident performance while maintaining excellent efficiency for a three-row SUV.

Specification Details
Engine 3.8L V6 GDI
Horsepower 291 hp @ 6,000 rpm
Torque 262 lb-ft @ 5,200 rpm
Transmission 8-speed automatic with SHIFTRONIC
Drivetrain Front-wheel drive or HTRAC AWD
Towing Capacity 5,000 lbs (with AWD and tow package)

The HTRAC all-wheel-drive system features multiple drive modes including Snow, Sport, and Comfort, automatically distributing power between the front and rear wheels for optimal traction in various conditions.

Impressive Fuel Efficiency

Despite its size and capability, the Palisade Calligraphy delivers competitive fuel economy for a three-row V6-powered SUV.

Configuration City MPG Highway MPG Combined MPG
FWD Models 19 26 22
AWD Models 19 24 21

Advanced Safety and Driver Assistance

The Palisade Calligraphy comes standard with Hyundai's comprehensive SmartSense safety suite, offering some of the most advanced driver assistance features in its class.

Hyundai SmartSense Features:

  • Forward Collision-Avoidance Assist with Pedestrian Detection
  • Lane Keeping Assist with Lane Following Assist
  • Smart Cruise Control with Stop & Go and Curve Control
  • Blind-Spot Collision-Avoidance Assist
  • Rear Cross-Traffic Collision-Avoidance Assist
  • Safe Exit Assist
  • Highway Driving Assist (semi-autonomous)
  • Surround View Monitor with Parking Guidance

The 2022-2023 Palisade earned top safety ratings, including a 5-Star Overall Safety Rating from NHTSA and Top Safety Pick+ designation from IIHS, making it one of the safest three-row SUVs available.

Family-Focused Technology

The Palisade Calligraphy is packed with family-friendly technology that enhances convenience, comfort, and entertainment for all occupants.

Technology Highlights:

  • 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster
  • 12.3-inch touchscreen navigation with split-screen
  • Wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
  • Wireless phone charging
  • 12-speaker Harman Kardon premium audio
  • In-car intercom system (Driver Talk)
  • Rear occupant alert system
  • Multiple USB charging ports (all three rows)

Practicality and Versatility

As a three-row family SUV, the Palisade Calligraphy excels in practicality with thoughtful features that make family life easier.

Family-Friendly Features:

  • One-Touch second-row slide and tilt
  • Power-folding third-row seats
  • 16 cupholders and multiple storage areas
  • Tri-zone automatic climate control
  • Heated and ventilated first and second-row seats
  • Generous cargo space (up to 86.4 cu ft with seats folded)
  • Standard roof rails for additional storage

Competitive Positioning and Value

The Palisade Calligraphy represents exceptional value in the premium three-row SUV segment, offering luxury features and amenities typically found in vehicles costing thousands more.

When compared to competitors like the Kia Telluride, Toyota Highlander, Honda Pilot, and luxury alternatives from Acura and Lexus, the Palisade Calligraphy offers:

  • More standard luxury features at a lower price point
  • Superior warranty coverage (5-year/60,000-mile basic, 10-year/100,000-mile powertrain)
  • More comprehensive standard safety features
  • Larger standard touchscreen and digital displays
  • More premium interior materials in Calligraphy trim
