2024 Hyundai Kona SEL: Premium Compact SUV Review

Discover the completely redesigned 2024 Hyundai Kona SEL - a bold and sophisticated compact SUV that combines striking design, advanced technology, and exceptional value in one impressive package.

Bold New Design: Urban Sophistication

The 2024 Hyundai Kona SEL represents a dramatic evolution in the compact SUV segment, featuring a completely redesigned platform with bold styling, increased dimensions, and premium features that elevate it above competitors. The second-generation Kona builds on the success of its predecessor with enhanced sophistication and urban appeal.

Built on Hyundai's new architecture, the 2024 Kona offers more interior space, improved driving dynamics, and advanced technology that make it one of the most compelling choices in the competitive subcompact SUV market.

Key SEL Features

  • Signature H-shaped LED daytime running lights
  • 17-inch alloy wheels with premium finish
  • Leatherette seating surfaces
  • Dual 12.3-inch panoramic display screens
  • Heated front seats and steering wheel
  • Wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto

Exterior Design: Standout Styling

The 2024 Kona features a bold and distinctive exterior design with Hyundai's new "H-shaped" LED lighting elements, muscular proportions, and sophisticated details that give it a premium presence on the road.

The SEL trim adds premium touches like 17-inch alloy wheels, body-color door handles and mirrors, and additional chrome accents that enhance the Kona's upscale appearance.

Exterior Design Features:

  • Signature H-shaped LED daytime running lights
  • Full LED headlights and taillights
  • 17-inch alloy wheels
  • Body-color door handles and mirrors
  • Chrome window surrounds
  • Available two-tone roof options

Interior: Tech-Focused Cabin

Inside, the 2024 Kona SEL offers a modern and technologically advanced cabin that belies its compact dimensions. The highlight is the dual 12.3-inch panoramic display that integrates the digital instrument cluster and infotainment system.

The interior design emphasizes both style and functionality, with premium materials, thoughtful storage solutions, and ample space for passengers and cargo despite the vehicle's compact footprint.

Interior Highlights:

  • Dual 12.3-inch panoramic displays
  • Leatherette seating surfaces
  • Heated front seats
  • Heated steering wheel
  • Automatic climate control
  • Wireless phone charging

Powertrain and Performance

The 2024 Kona SEL features an efficient and responsive powertrain that delivers confident performance while maintaining excellent fuel economy for daily commuting and urban adventures.

Specification Details
Engine 2.0L 4-cylinder MPI
Horsepower 147 hp @ 6,200 rpm
Torque 132 lb-ft @ 4,500 rpm
Transmission Intelligent Variable Transmission (IVT)
Drivetrain Front-wheel drive or available AWD
Fuel Economy 28 city / 32 highway MPG (FWD)

The available HTRAC all-wheel-drive system provides enhanced traction and stability in various driving conditions, making the Kona SEL capable in both urban environments and light off-road situations.

Impressive Fuel Efficiency

The 2024 Kona SEL delivers excellent fuel economy for a compact SUV, making it an ideal choice for daily commuting and urban driving.

Configuration City MPG Highway MPG Combined MPG
FWD Models 28 32 30
AWD Models 26 29 27

Advanced Safety and Driver Assistance

The 2024 Kona SEL comes standard with Hyundai's comprehensive SmartSense safety suite, offering advanced driver assistance features typically found in more expensive vehicles.

Hyundai SmartSense Features:

  • Forward Collision-Avoidance Assist with Pedestrian Detection
  • Lane Keeping Assist with Lane Following Assist
  • Smart Cruise Control with Stop & Go
  • Blind-Spot Collision-Avoidance Assist
  • Rear Cross-Traffic Collision-Avoidance Assist
  • Safe Exit Assist
  • Driver Attention Warning
  • Intelligent Speed Limit Assist

The 2024 Kona earned top safety ratings, including a 5-Star Overall Safety Rating from NHTSA and Top Safety Pick designation from IIHS, making it one of the safest compact SUVs available.

Technology and Connectivity

The 2024 Kona SEL is equipped with cutting-edge technology that enhances convenience, entertainment, and connectivity.

Technology Highlights:

  • Dual 12.3-inch panoramic displays
  • Wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
  • Wireless phone charging
  • Hyundai Blue Link connected car services
  • Multiple USB charging ports (Type-C and Type-A)
  • Available Bose premium audio system
  • Over-the-air update capability

Practicality and Versatility

Despite its compact dimensions, the 2024 Kona SEL offers impressive practicality with thoughtful features that enhance daily usability.

Practical Features:

  • Increased cargo space (25.5 cu ft behind rear seats)
  • 60/40 split-folding rear seats
  • Underfloor cargo storage
  • Multiple storage compartments
  • Rear seat USB charging ports
  • Available hands-free smart liftgate
  • Roof rails for additional storage

Competitive Positioning and Value

The 2024 Hyundai Kona SEL represents exceptional value in the competitive compact SUV segment, offering premium features and advanced technology at a competitive price point.

When compared to competitors like the Honda HR-V, Toyota Corolla Cross, Kia Seltos, and Mazda CX-30, the Kona SEL offers:

  • More comprehensive standard safety features
  • Larger standard touchscreen displays
  • More premium interior materials
  • Superior warranty coverage
  • More distinctive and modern design

Driving Experience and Comfort

The 2024 Kona SEL delivers a refined and comfortable driving experience with precise handling and a surprisingly quiet ride for a compact SUV. The well-tuned suspension effectively absorbs road imperfections while maintaining agile handling characteristics.

Available Drive Mode Select allows drivers to customize the vehicle's character, with Smart, Eco, Sport, and Normal modes that adjust throttle response, transmission mapping, and steering effort to suit different driving conditions and preferences.

Available Powertrain Options

Beyond the standard 2.0L engine in the SEL trim, the Kona lineup offers additional powertrain choices for different needs.

Powertrain Availability Key Features
2.0L MPI SE, SEL, N Line 147 hp, excellent fuel economy, IVT transmission
1.6L Turbo N Line, Limited 190 hp, 195 lb-ft torque, 8-speed automatic
