2023 Hyundai Santa Fe SEL: Premium Midsize SUV Review

Discover the 2023 Hyundai Santa Fe SEL - a sophisticated midsize SUV that combines premium design, advanced technology, and exceptional family versatility at an outstanding value.

Sophisticated Design: Modern SUV Styling

The 2023 Hyundai Santa Fe SEL represents the perfect balance of sophisticated styling and practical functionality in the competitive midsize SUV segment. With its bold design language and premium features, the Santa Fe continues to set standards for what a family SUV should be.

The 2023 model builds upon the successful redesign with refined styling elements, enhanced technology, and comprehensive standard features that make the SEL trim an exceptional value proposition.

Key SEL Features

  • Signature T-shaped LED daytime running lights
  • 18-inch alloy wheels with premium finish
  • Leatherette seating surfaces
  • 10.25-inch touchscreen infotainment system
  • Power liftgate with hands-free operation
  • Dual-zone automatic climate control

Exterior Design: Bold and Confident

The 2023 Santa Fe features a bold and confident exterior design that stands out in the crowded midsize SUV segment. The signature T-shaped LED daytime running lights, cascading grille, and muscular proportions create a premium appearance that commands attention.

The SEL trim adds premium touches like 18-inch alloy wheels, chrome accents, and additional lighting elements that elevate the Santa Fe's presence on the road.

Exterior Design Features:

  • Signature T-shaped LED daytime running lights
  • Cascading grille with chrome accents
  • Projector headlights with LED illumination
  • 18-inch alloy wheels
  • Chrome window surrounds and roof rails
  • Power-folding side mirrors with approach lights

Interior: Premium Comfort and Technology

Inside, the 2023 Santa Fe SEL offers a sophisticated and comfortable environment with premium materials and advanced technology. The cabin features thoughtful design elements and ample space for five passengers.

The interior design emphasizes both luxury and practicality, with easy-to-clean surfaces, abundant storage solutions, and flexible cargo configurations that adapt to various family needs.

Interior Highlights:

  • Leatherette seating surfaces
  • 10.25-inch touchscreen infotainment system
  • 8-inch digital instrument cluster
  • Heated front seats
  • Dual-zone automatic climate control
  • Wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto

Powertrain and Performance

The 2023 Santa Fe SEL features an efficient and responsive powertrain that delivers confident performance while maintaining excellent fuel economy.

Specification Details
Engine 2.5L 4-cylinder GDI
Horsepower 191 hp @ 6,100 rpm
Torque 181 lb-ft @ 4,000 rpm
Transmission 8-speed automatic with SHIFTRONIC
Drivetrain Front-wheel drive or HTRAC AWD
Towing Capacity 2,000 lbs (with AWD)

The HTRAC all-wheel-drive system features multiple drive modes including Snow, Sport, and Comfort, automatically distributing power between the front and rear wheels for optimal traction in various conditions.

Impressive Fuel Efficiency

The 2023 Santa Fe SEL delivers excellent fuel economy for a midsize SUV, making it an ideal choice for daily commuting and family adventures.

Configuration City MPG Highway MPG Combined MPG
FWD Models 25 28 26
AWD Models 24 27 25

Advanced Safety and Driver Assistance

The 2023 Santa Fe SEL comes standard with Hyundai's comprehensive SmartSense safety suite, offering advanced driver assistance features for enhanced peace of mind.

Hyundai SmartSense Features:

  • Forward Collision-Avoidance Assist with Pedestrian Detection
  • Lane Keeping Assist with Lane Following Assist
  • Smart Cruise Control with Stop & Go
  • Blind-Spot Collision-Avoidance Assist
  • Rear Cross-Traffic Collision-Avoidance Assist
  • Safe Exit Assist
  • Driver Attention Warning
  • Highway Driving Assist (semi-autonomous)

The 2023 Santa Fe earned top safety ratings, including a 5-Star Overall Safety Rating from NHTSA and Top Safety Pick designation from IIHS, making it one of the safest midsize SUVs available.

Family-Focused Technology

The 2023 Santa Fe SEL is equipped with family-friendly technology that enhances convenience, comfort, and entertainment for all occupants.

Technology Highlights:

  • 10.25-inch touchscreen navigation system
  • 8-inch digital instrument cluster
  • Wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
  • Wireless phone charging
  • Multiple USB charging ports (front and rear)
  • Bluetooth multi-connection
  • Hyundai Blue Link connected car services

Practicality and Versatility

As a family SUV, the 2023 Santa Fe SEL excels in practicality with thoughtful features that make daily life easier.

Family-Friendly Features:

  • 60/40 split-folding rear seats
  • Generous cargo space (up to 72.1 cu ft with seats folded)
  • Underfloor cargo storage
  • Multiple cupholders and storage areas
  • Rear seat USB charging ports
  • Standard roof rails for additional storage
  • Available hands-free power liftgate

Competitive Positioning and Value

The 2023 Hyundai Santa Fe SEL represents exceptional value in the competitive midsize SUV segment, offering premium features and amenities typically found in more expensive vehicles.

When compared to competitors like the Honda CR-V, Toyota RAV4, Ford Escape, and Nissan Rogue, the Santa Fe SEL offers:

  • More standard luxury features at a competitive price point
  • Superior warranty coverage (5-year/60,000-mile basic, 10-year/100,000-mile powertrain)
  • More comprehensive standard safety features
  • Larger standard touchscreen display
  • More premium interior materials

Driving Experience and Comfort

The 2023 Santa Fe SEL delivers a refined and comfortable driving experience with precise handling and a quiet, composed ride. The well-tuned suspension effectively absorbs road imperfections while maintaining confident handling characteristics.

Available Drive Mode Select allows drivers to customize the vehicle's character, with Smart, Eco, Sport, and Comfort modes that adjust throttle response, transmission mapping, and steering effort to suit different driving conditions and preferences.
