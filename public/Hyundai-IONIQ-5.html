Hyundai IONIQ 5: Electric Innovation & Design Revolution

Discover the groundbreaking Hyundai IONIQ 5 - the electric crossover that redefines sustainable mobility with its parametric pixel design, ultra-fast charging, and innovative interior space.

Revolutionary Design Philosophy

The Hyundai IONIQ 5 represents a paradigm shift in electric vehicle design, drawing inspiration from Hyundai's historic Pony Coupe concept while embracing futuristic aesthetics and sustainable materials.

Design Innovation Highlights

  • Parametric Pixels: Unique square-shaped design elements throughout exterior and interior
  • Clamshell Hood: Single panel design reducing gaps and improving aerodynamics
    • Sustainable Materials: Eco-friendly fabrics, recycled plastics, and plant-based leather alternatives
  • Universal Island: Movable center console for flexible interior space
  • Flat Floor: Maximized interior space thanks to E-GMP platform
  • Digital Side Mirrors: Available camera-based mirror system
