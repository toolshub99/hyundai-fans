2016 Hyundai Sonata: Refined Evolution with Premium Features

Discover the sophisticated 2016 Hyundai Sonata - featuring refined styling, advanced technology, and premium amenities that elevate the mid-size sedan experience while maintaining exceptional value.

Sophisticated Redesign: Mature Elegance

The 2016 Hyundai Sonata represents a significant evolution in Hyundai's design philosophy, moving from the dramatic "Fluidic Sculpture" of previous generations to a more sophisticated, mature aesthetic. This LF-generation Sonata features cleaner lines, bolder proportions, and premium details that compete directly with luxury sedans.

Building on the success of its predecessor, the 2016 model introduces enhanced refinement, improved technology, and a more premium driving experience. The design emphasizes elegance and sophistication while maintaining the Sonata's reputation for exceptional value.

Key Refinements for 2016

  • Sophisticated new exterior design with cascading grille
  • Enhanced interior with premium materials and craftsmanship
  • Advanced driver assistance systems available
  • Improved ride quality and noise isolation
  • Latest infotainment technology with larger displays
  • Expanded standard feature content

Exterior Design: Confident and Premium

The 2016 Sonata features a completely redesigned exterior that exudes confidence and sophistication. The new cascading grille, sleek LED lighting elements, and clean body lines create a premium appearance that stands out in the competitive mid-size sedan segment.

Key design elements include a wider stance, more pronounced shoulder lines, and elegant proportions that give the Sonata a substantial, upscale presence. Available LED headlights and taillights add both style and functionality, while aerodynamic enhancements improve both efficiency and refinement.

Exterior Design Features:

  • Signature cascading grille design
  • Available LED headlights and daytime running lights
  • Sleek profile with chrome accents
  • Available 17-inch and 18-inch alloy wheels
  • Integrated rear spoiler on sport models
  • Dual exhaust tips on Sport and Limited trims

Interior: Luxury-Inspired Cabin

Inside, the 2016 Sonata offers a remarkably upscale environment with premium materials, thoughtful ergonomics, and advanced technology. The cabin features soft-touch surfaces, available leather upholstery, and meticulous attention to detail throughout.

The interior space is both spacious and comfortable, with best-in-class rear legroom and generous cargo capacity. Advanced noise-reduction technologies create a quiet, serene cabin that rivals more expensive luxury vehicles.

Interior Highlights:

  • Premium soft-touch dashboard and door panels
  • Available leather-trimmed seats with heating and ventilation
  • Dual-zone automatic climate control
  • Available panoramic sunroof
  • Generous 16.3 cubic feet trunk space
  • Advanced Blue Link connected car system

Advanced Powertrain Options

The 2016 Sonata offers a range of efficient and powerful engine options, including an all-new turbocharged engine that delivers impressive performance without sacrificing fuel economy.

Engine Horsepower Torque Transmission
2.4L GDI 4-cylinder 185 hp 178 lb-ft 6-speed automatic
1.6L Turbo 4-cylinder (Eco) 178 hp 195 lb-ft 7-speed dual-clutch automatic
2.0L Turbo 4-cylinder (Sport/Limited) 245 hp 260 lb-ft 6-speed automatic

The available 7-speed dual-clutch transmission in the Eco model provides both smooth operation and exceptional fuel efficiency, while the 2.0L turbo engine delivers sports sedan-level performance.

Exceptional Fuel Efficiency

The 2016 Sonata delivers impressive fuel economy across all engine options, with the Eco model achieving hybrid-like efficiency without the complexity of hybrid technology.

Advanced Safety and Driver Assistance

The 2016 Sonata offers an impressive suite of available safety technologies that were typically reserved for luxury vehicles, including Hyundai's first autonomous emergency braking system.

Available Safety Features:

  • Automatic Emergency Braking with Pedestrian Detection
  • Blind Spot Detection with Rear Cross-Traffic Alert
  • Lane Departure Warning with Lane Keep Assist
  • Smart Cruise Control with full stop capability
  • Automatic High Beam Assist
  • Surround View Monitor system

The 2016 Sonata earned top safety ratings, including a 5-Star Overall Safety Rating from NHTSA and Top Safety Pick+ designation from IIHS when equipped with specific safety features.

Technology and Connectivity

The 2016 Sonata features advanced technology that keeps drivers connected and entertained, including Apple CarPlay and Android Auto integration on higher trims.

Technology Features:

  • Available 8-inch touchscreen navigation
  • Apple CarPlay and Android Auto integration
  • Hyundai Blue Link connected car system
  • Available wireless phone charging
  • Premium Infinity audio with Clari-Fi technology
  • HD Radio and SiriusXM capability

The Premium Value Proposition

The 2016 Hyundai Sonata successfully bridges the gap between mainstream and luxury, offering premium features, sophisticated styling, and advanced technology at a fraction of the cost of European luxury sedans.

This generation Sonata demonstrates Hyundai's commitment to continuous improvement and customer satisfaction, building on the brand's reputation for quality, reliability, and exceptional value. The combination of style, technology, and affordability makes the 2016 Sonata one of the most compelling choices in the mid-size sedan segment.
