2008 Hyundai Sonata: Complete Review, Fuel Economy, and Value Analysis

Discover the refined 2008 Hyundai Sonata with its updated styling, improved performance, and exceptional value that continues to compete strongly in the midsize sedan segment.

2008 Hyundai Sonata: Refined and Improved

The 2008 Hyundai Sonata builds upon the successful redesign from 2006 with subtle refinements and additional features. This midsize sedan continues to offer Hyundai's signature combination of quality, value, and comprehensive standard equipment that makes it a compelling choice in a competitive segment.

Maintaining the NF platform introduced in 2006, the 2008 Sonata delivers comfortable driving dynamics, enhanced safety features, and a spacious interior with quality materials that belie its affordable price point.

Key Features of the 2008 Model

  • Refreshed exterior styling with new grille design
  • Enhanced interior materials and finishes
  • Available 3.3L V6 engine with 234 horsepower
  • Standard electronic stability control across all trims
  • Six standard airbags for comprehensive protection
  • Spacious cabin with generous rear legroom

Hyundai Sonata SE: Sporty Performance and Style

The Hyundai Sonata SE trim offers a sportier alternative to the base GL and premium Limited models. With unique styling cues and performance enhancements, the SE delivers a more engaging driving experience while maintaining the Sonata's reputation for value.

SE Exclusive Features:

  • Sport-tuned suspension for improved handling
  • 17-inch alloy wheels with performance tires
  • Leather-wrapped steering wheel with audio controls
  • Unique front grille and body styling
  • Fog lights
  • Sport seats with additional bolstering

Impressive Fuel Economy: Hyundai Sonata MPG

The 2008 Sonata delivers competitive fuel efficiency for a midsize sedan, with the base four-cylinder engine providing an excellent balance of power and economy. The available V6 model offers strong performance while maintaining reasonable fuel consumption.

Engine/Trim City MPG Highway MPG Combined MPG
2.4L 4-cylinder (GL, SE) 22 32 26
3.3L V6 (Limited) 19 28 22

The Sonata's fuel economy numbers remain competitive for its class in 2008, with the four-cylinder model offering particularly good efficiency for daily commuting and highway driving.

2008 Hyundai Sonata Pricing & Trim Levels

Advanced Technology & Safety Features

The 2008 Sonata continues to offer comprehensive standard safety features that were often optional on competitors. Electronic Stability Control and six airbags remain standard across all trims - a significant advantage in its class.

Standard Safety Features:

  • Electronic Stability Control (ESC)
  • Traction Control System (TCS)
  • Anti-lock Braking System (ABS)
  • Six airbags (front, side, curtain)
  • Active front head restraints
  • Tire pressure monitoring system

Available premium features included leather upholstery, heated seats, a power sunroof, and an upgraded Infinity audio system with CD changer.

Why Choose the 2008 Hyundai Sonata?

The 2008 Hyundai Sonata stands out in the midsize sedan segment with its refined styling, comprehensive standard safety features, and exceptional value. Its combination of attractive design, comfortable interior, and competitive pricing makes it a compelling choice for sedan shoppers in 2008.

Whether you're considering the well-equipped base GL model, the sporty SE, or the premium Limited with V6 power, the Sonata delivers a quality experience that's difficult to match in its price range.
