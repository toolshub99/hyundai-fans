Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions we receive

How can I submit a vehicle review?

We welcome owner reviews! Please use our contact form and select "Content Suggestion" as the subject. Include details about your Hyundai model, year, and your experience.

Are you affiliated with Hyundai Motor Company?

No, HyundaiFans is an independent fan site and resource. We are not affiliated with Hyundai Motor Company, though we strive to provide accurate information about their vehicles.

Can I advertise on HyundaiFans?

Yes, we offer advertising opportunities for relevant automotive products and services. Please contact us with "Partnership Opportunity" as the subject for more information.

How often is your vehicle information updated?

We update our content regularly as new information becomes available from Hyundai. Major model updates and new releases are typically reflected on our site within 24-48 hours of official announcements.

Do you provide buying advice?

While we provide comprehensive vehicle information and comparisons, we recommend consulting with authorized Hyundai dealers for personalized buying advice and test drives.

Can I suggest a Hyundai model to feature?

Absolutely! We're always interested in hearing which models our community wants to learn more about. Use our contact form with "Content Suggestion" as the subject.
