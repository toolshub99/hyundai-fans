2022 Hyundai Palisade: Premium Three-Row SUV Review

Discover the 2022 Hyundai Palisade - a sophisticated three-row SUV that combines premium design, advanced technology, and exceptional family versatility at an outstanding value.

Sophisticated Design: Commanding Road Presence

The 2022 Hyundai Palisade represents the pinnacle of Hyundai's SUV design philosophy, offering a perfect blend of sophisticated styling, premium craftsmanship, and family-friendly functionality. As Hyundai's flagship SUV, the Palisade competes directly with established players in the competitive three-row SUV segment.

The 2022 model continues the successful formula that made the Palisade an instant hit, featuring refined styling, comprehensive standard features, and thoughtful design elements that prioritize both luxury and practicality.

Key Design Features

  • Signature cascading chrome grille design
  • Full LED lighting front and rear
  • Available 20-inch alloy wheels
  • Premium chrome accents and badging
  • Power-folding side mirrors with approach lights
  • Sleek profile with commanding road presence

Exterior Design: Bold and Refined

The 2022 Palisade features a bold and sophisticated exterior design that commands attention on the road. The signature cascading grille, full LED lighting, and elegant proportions create a premium appearance that belies its accessible price point.

The design emphasizes both style and functionality, with careful attention to aerodynamics and practical features like roof rails, generous ground clearance, and available HTRAC all-wheel drive for enhanced capability.

Exterior Design Features:

  • Signature cascading chrome grille
  • Full LED headlights and daytime running lights
  • LED taillights with unique signature lighting
  • Available 18-inch and 20-inch alloy wheels
  • Chrome window surrounds and roof rails
  • Power liftgate with hands-free operation

Interior: Premium Family Sanctuary

Inside, the 2022 Palisade offers a truly premium environment that comfortably accommodates up to eight passengers. The cabin features high-quality materials, thoughtful details, and advanced technology throughout all three rows.

The interior design prioritizes both luxury and practicality, with easy-to-clean surfaces, abundant storage solutions, and flexible seating configurations that adapt to various family needs.

Interior Highlights:

  • Premium leather seating surfaces (higher trims)
  • 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster (Limited, Calligraphy)
  • 10.25-inch touchscreen infotainment system
  • Heated and ventilated front seats
  • Tri-zone automatic climate control
  • Available 12-speaker Harman Kardon audio system

Powertrain and Performance

The 2022 Palisade features a refined powertrain that delivers smooth, confident performance while maintaining excellent efficiency for a three-row SUV.

Specification Details
Engine 3.8L V6 GDI
Horsepower 291 hp @ 6,000 rpm
Torque 262 lb-ft @ 5,200 rpm
Transmission 8-speed automatic with SHIFTRONIC
Drivetrain Front-wheel drive or HTRAC AWD
Towing Capacity 5,000 lbs (with AWD and tow package)

The HTRAC all-wheel-drive system features multiple drive modes including Snow, Sport, and Comfort, automatically distributing power between the front and rear wheels for optimal traction in various conditions.

Impressive Fuel Efficiency

Despite its size and capability, the 2022 Palisade delivers competitive fuel economy for a three-row V6-powered SUV.

Configuration City MPG Highway MPG Combined MPG
FWD Models 19 26 22
AWD Models 19 24 21

Advanced Safety and Driver Assistance

The 2022 Palisade comes standard with Hyundai's comprehensive SmartSense safety suite, offering some of the most advanced driver assistance features in its class.

Hyundai SmartSense Features:

  • Forward Collision-Avoidance Assist with Pedestrian Detection
  • Lane Keeping Assist with Lane Following Assist
  • Smart Cruise Control with Stop & Go
  • Blind-Spot Collision-Avoidance Assist
  • Rear Cross-Traffic Collision-Avoidance Assist
  • Safe Exit Assist
  • Driver Attention Warning
  • Highway Driving Assist (semi-autonomous)

The 2022 Palisade earned top safety ratings, including a 5-Star Overall Safety Rating from NHTSA and Top Safety Pick+ designation from IIHS, making it one of the safest three-row SUVs available.

Family-Focused Technology

The 2022 Palisade is packed with family-friendly technology that enhances convenience, comfort, and entertainment for all occupants.

Technology Highlights:

  • 10.25-inch touchscreen navigation system
  • 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster (higher trims)
  • Wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
  • Wireless phone charging
  • Available 12-speaker Harman Kardon premium audio
  • In-car intercom system (Driver Talk)
  • Rear occupant alert system
  • Multiple USB charging ports (all three rows)

Practicality and Versatility

As a three-row family SUV, the 2022 Palisade excels in practicality with thoughtful features that make family life easier.

Family-Friendly Features:

  • One-Touch second-row slide and tilt
  • Power-folding third-row seats
  • 16 cupholders and multiple storage areas
  • Tri-zone automatic climate control
  • Heated and ventilated front seats
  • Generous cargo space (up to 86.4 cu ft with seats folded)
  • Standard roof rails for additional storage

Competitive Positioning and Value

The 2022 Hyundai Palisade represents exceptional value in the competitive three-row SUV segment, offering premium features and amenities typically found in more expensive vehicles.

When compared to competitors like the Toyota Highlander, Honda Pilot, Ford Explorer, and Kia Telluride, the Palisade offers:

  • More standard luxury features at a competitive price point
  • Superior warranty coverage (5-year/60,000-mile basic, 10-year/100,000-mile powertrain)
  • More comprehensive standard safety features
  • Larger standard touchscreen and available digital displays
  • More premium interior materials in higher trims

Driving Experience and Comfort

The 2022 Palisade delivers a refined and comfortable driving experience with precise handling and a quiet, composed ride. The well-tuned suspension effectively absorbs road imperfections while maintaining confident handling characteristics.

Available Drive Mode Select allows drivers to customize the vehicle's character, with Smart, Eco, Sport, and Comfort modes that adjust throttle response, transmission mapping, and steering effort to suit different driving conditions and preferences.
