Hyundai IONIQ 6: Electric Streamliner - Complete Guide

Discover the groundbreaking Hyundai IONIQ 6 - the electric streamliner that redefines aerodynamic efficiency with its stunning design, record-breaking range, and innovative technology.

Revolutionary Streamliner Design

The Hyundai IONIQ 6 represents a paradigm shift in electric vehicle design, featuring an ultra-aerodynamic "streamliner" shape that combines retro-futuristic styling with cutting-edge efficiency.

Design Innovation Highlights

  • Record Aerodynamics: 0.21 Cd - one of the most aerodynamic production cars
  • Parametric Pixels: Signature lighting elements throughout exterior
  • Streamliner Profile: Smooth, flowing lines inspired by aircraft design
  • Digital Side Mirrors: Available camera-based system for reduced drag
  • Active Air Flaps: Automatically adjust for optimal airflow
  • Eco-Friendly Materials: Sustainable fabrics and recycled components
