Hyundai Santa Fe Configurations: Complete Trim Level Guide

Explore all Hyundai Santa Fe configurations with detailed comparisons of trim levels, available packages, and optional features to help you choose the perfect midsize SUV.

Understanding Santa Fe Configurations

The Hyundai Santa Fe offers a comprehensive range of configurations designed to meet diverse customer needs, from budget-conscious buyers to those seeking premium luxury features. With multiple trim levels, available packages, and optional equipment, understanding the Santa Fe's configuration options is key to finding your perfect SUV.

This guide covers the complete Santa Fe lineup, highlighting the key differences between trim levels and available packages to help you make an informed decision.

Configuration Overview

  • Five main trim levels: SE, SEL, XRT, Limited, Calligraphy
  • Available front-wheel drive or HTRAC all-wheel drive
  • Multiple engine options including hybrid and plug-in hybrid
  • Various package options for enhanced features
  • Different wheel sizes and exterior styling elements
  • Progressive technology and luxury features across trims

Base Configuration: SE Trim

The SE trim serves as the entry point to the Santa Fe lineup, offering impressive standard features while maintaining an accessible price point.

SE Standard Features:

  • 2.5L 4-cylinder engine with 191 horsepower
  • 17-inch alloy wheels
  • 8-inch touchscreen infotainment system
  • Apple CarPlay and Android Auto compatibility
  • Hyundai SmartSense safety suite
  • Forward Collision-Avoidance Assist
  • Lane Keeping Assist
  • Manual climate control
  • Fabric seating surfaces

Popular Mid-Range: SEL Trim

The SEL trim builds upon the SE foundation with additional comfort, convenience, and technology features that make it one of the most popular Santa Fe configurations.

SEL Standard Features (in addition to SE):

  • 18-inch alloy wheels
  • Power liftgate
  • Dual-zone automatic climate control
  • Leatherette seating surfaces
  • Heated front seats
  • Blind-Spot Collision-Avoidance Assist
  • Rear Cross-Traffic Collision-Avoidance Assist
  • Power driver seat with lumbar support
  • Rear USB charging ports

Adventure-Ready: XRT Trim

The XRT trim adds rugged styling elements and enhanced capability for buyers seeking a more adventurous Santa Fe configuration.

XRT Standard Features (in addition to SEL):

  • Unique XRT exterior styling
  • 18-inch dark gray alloy wheels
  • Roof rails with crossbars
  • All-weather floor mats
  • Unique front and rear skid plates
  • Black exterior accents
  • Paddle shifters
  • HTRAC all-wheel drive standard

Premium Luxury: Limited Trim

The Limited trim elevates the Santa Fe experience with premium materials, advanced technology, and luxury amenities.

Limited Standard Features (in addition to XRT):

  • Leather seating surfaces
  • Ventilated front seats
  • 10.25-inch touchscreen navigation
  • 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster
  • Harmon Kardon premium audio system
  • Surround View Monitor
  • Rain-sensing wipers
  • Panoramic sunroof
  • Hands-free smart liftgate

Top-Tier Luxury: Calligraphy Trim

The Calligraphy trim represents the pinnacle of Santa Fe luxury, offering the most premium features and sophisticated styling.

Calligraphy Standard Features (in addition to Limited):

  • Premium Nappa leather seating
  • Quilted leather door panel inserts
  • Suede-like headliner
  • 20-inch alloy wheels
  • Blind View Monitor
  • Remote Smart Parking Assist
  • Heated second-row seats
  • Premium ambient lighting
  • Chrome exterior accents

Powertrain Configurations

The Santa Fe offers multiple powertrain options to suit different driving preferences and efficiency needs.

Standard Gasoline

All Trims
  • 2.5L 4-cylinder GDI
  • 191 horsepower
  • 181 lb-ft torque
  • 8-speed automatic
  • FWD or AWD available
  • 24 city / 29 highway MPG (AWD)

Hybrid

SEL, Limited, Calligraphy
  • 1.6L Turbo Hybrid
  • 226 combined horsepower
  • 258 lb-ft torque
  • 6-speed automatic
  • Standard AWD
  • 33 city / 30 highway MPG

Plug-in Hybrid

Limited, Calligraphy
  • 1.6L Turbo PHEV
  • 261 combined horsepower
  • 258 lb-ft torque
  • 6-speed automatic
  • Standard AWD
  • 76 MPGe combined
  • 31 miles electric range

Drivetrain Options

All Santa Fe configurations offer a choice between front-wheel drive and HTRAC all-wheel drive systems.

Front-Wheel Drive (FWD):

  • Standard on SE, SEL, Limited, Calligraphy trims
  • Better fuel economy
  • Lower purchase price
  • Ideal for primarily urban and highway driving

HTRAC All-Wheel Drive (AWD):

  • Standard on XRT and hybrid models
  • Available on all other trims
  • Enhanced traction in adverse conditions
  • Multiple drive modes (Comfort, Sport, Smart, Snow)
  • Improved handling and stability

Color and Interior Options

The Santa Fe offers a variety of exterior colors and interior themes to personalize your vehicle.

Category Options Available Notes
Exterior Colors 8-10 options including Hampton Gray, Phantom Black, Atlas White, Sierra Burgundy Some colors may be trim-specific or require additional cost
Interior Colors Black, Gray, Beige, Burgundy (depending on trim) Higher trims offer more color choices and premium materials
Wheel Options 17-inch, 18-inch, 19-inch, 20-inch alloys Wheel size increases with trim level

Configuration Recommendations

Based on different needs and budgets, here are our recommended Santa Fe configurations:

Best Value: SEL with Premium Package

Offers an excellent balance of features and price, including the larger touchscreen, digital cluster, and additional comfort features without the premium cost of higher trims.

Family Favorite: Limited with Technology Package

Provides all the luxury and safety features families need, including advanced driver assistance systems, premium audio, and comfort amenities for long journeys.

Adventure Ready: XRT with Tow Package

Perfect for outdoor enthusiasts with rugged styling, standard AWD, and increased towing capacity for trailers or outdoor gear.

Ultimate Luxury: Calligraphy with All Options

The top-of-the-line configuration with every available feature for those seeking the ultimate in comfort, technology, and premium materials.
